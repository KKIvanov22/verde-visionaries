Keep your beaches pretty

This beautiful beach in Hawaii doesn't look as good anymore. And that can happen to any beach out there! Irresponsible tourism, sloth and public indecency are the main reasons why beaches often get polluted beyond recognition.

We encourage tourists and those native to beachside settlements to avoid doing such things. And if you and your friends organize enough people, you can even clean a whole beach and make it beautiful again.
