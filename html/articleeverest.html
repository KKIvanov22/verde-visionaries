Keep Mt. Everest clean

Believe it or not, Mt. Everest is actually the world's most polluted mountain. Every year, millions of people visit it and leave their trash behind for one reason or another. Sometimes, climbers just have other priorities and that's pretty reasonable given how dangerous it is up there. We encourage the visitors of this magical place (and anywhere else) to avoid doing so if possible.
